Pacman
 Create Pacman all over your browser!
Load the page https://nater14.github.io/Pacman/index.html (or click on the link in the Navigation bar
on the top of this page) and then hit the button that says Add Pacman. You can click this as many times
as you like. It continues to spawn Pacmans. As soon as you've created as many as you want click start game.
This will start all the pacmans moving in random directions. When they hit the edges they bounce back! Pro
tip, you can continue to create pacman even after you've hit start game. Also the more you hit start game
the quicker the pacman go!
